Community

Leadership

For two years, I served on the executive cabinet of Chi Sigma Alpha, an Asian-interest sorority under United Greek Council.

We were established at UW Seattle in 2002.

During my leadership, I mitigated and resolved conflicts unique to multicultural greek life. I had to make decisions around standardizing new member education, developing new fair rules and fines that reflect and adapt to our needs, solving interpersonal conflicts without bias, and keeping positive greek community relations. As a result, our house has doubled over from 13 to 45 sisters!
